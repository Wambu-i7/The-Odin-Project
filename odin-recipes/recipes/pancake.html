Pancakes Recipe

Pancakes

Below is a recipe for making the best pancakes. You will need a few ingredients to make this yummy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of self-raising flour
  • Honey
  • 2tbsp of Sugar for taste
  • quarter cup of oil
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 2tbsp of Salt for taste
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the dry ingredients.(flour, salt and sugar)
  2. Mix the dry ingredient together.
  3. Add water as you whisk the mixture.
  4. When the mixture is light and fluffy, add the eggs.
  5. Continue whisking till the mixture is consistent.
  6. Scope some of the mixture with a laddle and spread it in a pan. Continue till the mixture is done.
  7. Pour some honey and some toppings of choice on the pancakes and enjoy.
