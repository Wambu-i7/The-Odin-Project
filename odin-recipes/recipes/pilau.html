Pilau Recipe

Pilau

Below is a recipe for making the best pilau dish. You will need a few ingredients to make this yummy dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • Boiled half a kg of beef
  • A satchet of pilau masala
  • Dark Soysauce
  • 3 big chopped red onions
  • quarter cup of oil
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt for taste
  • 3 big chopped tomatoes
  • A satchet of tomato paste

Steps

  1. In a pot, put the chopped onions.
  2. Add oil to the onions and allow them to fry till brown.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and the tomatoe paste and stirr till the tomatoes are fully mashed.
  4. Put the pilau masala as you stirr.
  5. Add the boiled beef as you continue to stirr.
  6. Add salt and a quarter cup of the dark soysauce and continue stirring.
  7. When the meat is tenderized and completely mixed up with the rest of the ingredients, add the rice and stirr.
  8. Finally add the water, stirr, reduce heat and cover the pot with the lid and wait.
  9. When the water is fully dried, serve while hot and enjoy.
